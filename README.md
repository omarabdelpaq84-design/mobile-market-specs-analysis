# Mobile Market Specification & Pricing Analysis 📱📊

## Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive look at the smartphone market. By analyzing data from 379 different mobile models, the project identifies key price drivers and performance benchmarks. It transforms raw technical specifications into strategic market insights, helping to understand brand positioning and hardware value.

## Project Workflow
The repository is organized to showcase the complete data transformation process:

1.  **Raw Data (`Data Before Cleaning`):** Initial dataset containing messy strings of technical specs (Connectivity, Processor, Battery, Camera) and unformatted pricing.
2.  **Processed Data (`Data After Cleaning`):** The cleaned dataset where hardware features are separated into distinct columns (e.g., NFC check, IR Blaster check, Processor Company) for precise filtering.
3.  **Core Metrics (`Measures`):** Statistical aggregations including:
    * **Brand Performance:** Revenue and model counts for top companies like Xiaomi, Samsung, Apple, and OnePlus.
    * **Hardware Distribution:** Analysis of Processor companies (Snapdragon, Dimensity, Helio, Exynos, Tensor) and their market share.
    * **Pricing Averages:** Identification of the average market price (~43.5K) and total revenue (~16.5M).
4.  **Strategic Insights (`Data Story` & `DashBoard`):** A narrative approach to the data, summarizing the "story" behind the numbers and providing a high-level visual dashboard of KPIs.

## Key Insights Analyzed
- **Feature Correlation:** How specific features like 5G connectivity, NFC, and IR Blasters affect the final retail price.
- **Brand Landscape:** Comparing high-volume brands (Xiaomi, Poco) against premium market leaders.
- **Hardware Trends:** The dominance of specific processor architectures and battery capacities across different price segments.
- **Consumer Ratings:** Analyzing average ratings (4.4/5) to see if higher prices correlate with better user satisfaction.

## Tools Used
- **Microsoft Excel:** For advanced data cleaning (Power Query) and categorical modeling.
- **Data Storytelling:** Translating statistical measures into business narratives.

## How to Explore
- Check `Data After Cleaning.csv` for the structured mobile hardware data.
- Refer to `DashBorad.csv` for a summary of the total market revenue and company counts.
